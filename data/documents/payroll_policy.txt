PAYROLL & COMPENSATION POLICY — Acme Technologies Pvt Ltd
============================================================

1. SALARY CYCLE AND DISBURSEMENT
Salaries are processed on a monthly basis and credited to the employee's
registered bank account on the last working day of each month. If the
last working day falls on a weekend or public holiday, salary will be
credited on the preceding working day.

2. SALARY STRUCTURE
Every employee's salary (Cost to Company / CTC) is divided into the
following components:
- Basic Pay: 40% of CTC
- House Rent Allowance (HRA): 20% of Basic Pay
- Special Allowance: balancing component
- Provident Fund (PF) Employer Contribution: 12% of Basic Pay
- Gratuity: as per the Payment of Gratuity Act, accrued but paid only on
  separation after 5 years of continuous service
- Performance Bonus: variable, paid annually based on appraisal rating

3. PAYSLIPS AND TAX DOCUMENTS
Payslips are generated automatically and available for download from the
HR portal by the 2nd working day of every month. Form 16 (annual tax
certificate) is issued by the end of June each financial year.

4. INCOME TAX AND DEDUCTIONS
Income tax (TDS) is deducted at source as per the income tax slab
declared by the employee. Employees must submit investment declarations
(Section 80C, 80D, HRA proofs, etc.) by 15th December of each financial
year to avoid excess tax deduction in the last quarter.

5. REIMBURSEMENTS
- Internet/mobile reimbursement: up to ₹1,500 per month for employees
  working in a hybrid or remote capacity, on submission of bills.
- Travel reimbursement for official work: actuals, with prior manager
  approval and original bills/e-receipts submitted within 30 days.
- Reimbursement claims older than 60 days will not be processed.

6. SALARY REVISIONS AND PROMOTIONS
Salary revisions are typically reviewed once a year during the annual
appraisal cycle (April). Off-cycle revisions may happen in case of an
internal promotion, role change, or retention case, subject to manager
and HR Business Partner approval.

7. FULL AND FINAL SETTLEMENT (F&F)
On resignation or termination, the Full and Final settlement — including
pending salary, earned leave encashment, gratuity (if eligible), and any
deductions for notice period shortfall or company property not returned —
will be processed within 45 days of the last working day.

8. LOANS AND ADVANCES
Employees who have completed 1 year of service may request a salary
advance of up to 1 month's basic pay, recoverable in equal installments
over a maximum of 6 months, subject to HR and Finance approval.

9. BANK DETAILS AND ERRORS
Employees are responsible for keeping their bank account details updated
in the HR portal. The company is not liable for delays in salary credit
caused by incorrect or outdated bank information provided by the
employee.

10. CONTACT
For payslip issues, tax declaration help, or reimbursement queries,
employees can raise a ticket via the chatbot's "Escalate to HR" option or
email payroll@acmetech.example.com.
