WORK FROM HOME (WFH) & REMOTE WORK POLICY — Acme Technologies Pvt Ltd
========================================================================

1. ELIGIBILITY
This policy applies to all confirmed employees whose role does not
require mandatory on-site presence (e.g., manufacturing, security,
front-desk, lab operations). Employees on probation may request WFH only
with explicit manager approval, evaluated case by case.

2. HYBRID WORK MODEL
The company follows a hybrid work model:
- Monday, Tuesday, Thursday: Mandatory in-office days for all
  office-based teams.
- Wednesday and Friday: Optional Work From Home.
Teams may follow a different in-office schedule if approved by their
department head and published on the internal wiki.

3. FULLY REMOTE EMPLOYEES
Employees hired explicitly as "Remote" (as stated in their offer letter)
are exempt from the in-office requirement but are expected to visit the
nearest office at least once per quarter for team connect sessions, at
company expense.

4. REQUESTING ADDITIONAL WFH DAYS
For WFH beyond the standard policy (e.g., due to medical reasons, family
emergency, or relocation), employees must submit a request through the
HR portal at least 2 working days in advance, along with a reason. Such
requests are approved at the discretion of the reporting manager.

5. WORK HOURS AND AVAILABILITY
Regardless of work location, employees are expected to be available
during core hours: 10:30 AM to 4:30 PM IST. Employees must keep their
calendar and chat status updated to reflect their availability.

6. EQUIPMENT AND INTERNET
The company provides a laptop and basic peripherals for remote work.
Employees working remotely for more than 2 days a week are eligible for
the internet reimbursement described in the Payroll & Compensation
Policy (up to ₹1,500/month).

7. DATA SECURITY WHILE WORKING REMOTELY
- Company laptops must always be used with VPN enabled when accessing
  internal systems from outside the office network.
- Employees must not work on shared/public Wi-Fi for tasks involving
  confidential company or customer data.
- Screens must be locked when away from the device, and confidential
  conversations should not happen in public spaces.

8. PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS
Working remotely does not change performance expectations. Deliverables,
deadlines, and meeting attendance are evaluated the same way regardless
of work location. Repeated unavailability during core hours while marked
as WFH may be treated as a conduct issue.

9. WORKING FROM A DIFFERENT CITY OR COUNTRY
Employees wishing to work temporarily from a different city for more than
2 weeks (e.g., visiting family) must inform HR for tax, compliance, and
insurance purposes. Working from outside India requires prior written
approval from HR and Legal due to data protection and tax regulations.

10. CONTACT
For WFH requests beyond standard policy or equipment issues, raise a
ticket via the chatbot's "Escalate to HR" option or email
peopleops@acmetech.example.com.
