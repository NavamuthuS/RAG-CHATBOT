CODE OF CONDUCT POLICY — Acme Technologies Pvt Ltd
=====================================================

1. PURPOSE
This Code of Conduct sets out the standards of behaviour expected from
every employee, contractor, and intern at Acme Technologies, to maintain
a safe, respectful, and professional workplace.

2. PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOUR
Employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues, clients, and vendors with respect and courtesy.
- Communicate professionally in all written and verbal interactions,
  including on internal chat tools and email.
- Avoid discrimination or harassment based on gender, religion, caste,
  disability, sexual orientation, age, or any other protected
  characteristic.

3. ANTI-HARASSMENT AND POSH POLICY
Acme Technologies has zero tolerance for sexual harassment in the
workplace, in line with the Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) Act,
2013. Any employee facing or witnessing harassment can report it
confidentially to the Internal Committee (IC) or escalate directly
through the HR chatbot. Retaliation against a complainant or witness is
strictly prohibited and will itself be treated as a serious violation.

4. CONFLICT OF INTEREST
Employees must disclose any situation where personal interests (e.g.,
financial interest in a vendor, hiring a relative, side business in a
competing space) could conflict with the company's interests. Undisclosed
conflicts of interest may lead to disciplinary action.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY AND DATA PROTECTION
- All employees must protect confidential company information, customer
  data, and intellectual property, both during and after employment.
- Sharing confidential information with unauthorized persons, on social
  media, or with competitors is a serious violation and may lead to
  termination and legal action.
- Employees must follow the company's Data Security guidelines, including
  using VPN for remote access (see WFH Policy) and not storing company
  data on personal devices or personal cloud storage.

6. USE OF COMPANY ASSETS
Company assets (laptops, software licenses, official email, etc.) must be
used primarily for work purposes. Limited personal use is permitted as
long as it does not interfere with work, violate any law, or involve
illegal downloads, harassment, or accessing inappropriate content.

7. ANTI-BRIBERY AND GIFTS
Employees must not accept or offer bribes, kickbacks, or gifts above
₹2,000 in value from/to vendors, clients, or partners in connection with
business dealings. Gifts above this value must be disclosed to HR and may
need to be declined or surrendered.

8. SUBSTANCE USE
Employees must not be under the influence of alcohol or any intoxicating
substance during work hours or while on office/client premises. Violation
may lead to immediate suspension pending investigation.

9. DRESS CODE
The company follows a "smart casual" dress code on regular days. Formal
business attire is expected on days with client visits, leadership town
halls, or official events, as communicated in advance by the respective
team or HR.

10. DISCIPLINARY PROCESS
Violations of this Code of Conduct will be investigated by HR, and may
result in a verbal warning, written warning, performance improvement
plan, suspension, or termination, depending on the severity and history
of the violation. Employees under investigation will be given a fair
opportunity to respond before any action is taken.

11. REPORTING A CONCERN
Any employee who witnesses or experiences a violation of this Code can
report it confidentially using the chatbot's "Escalate to HR" button, or
by emailing conduct@acmetech.example.com. All reports are handled with
strict confidentiality, and retaliation against anyone reporting in good
faith is strictly prohibited.
